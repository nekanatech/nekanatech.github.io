The syllogism

By definition a capitalistic society requires three components: a society in where firms are based on privately ownership of the means of production, a society in where firms are based on exploiting workers and wage labour, and a society in where firms are based on accumulating as much capital as possible (or, the profit motive).

For any given society, if said society is capitalistic, then for all the inhabitants of that society, if said inhabitant can be arbitrarily categorised, then that inhabitant will suffer from prejudice and the negative effects of power imbalance. America meets all three components to be called a capitalistic society, and ethnic minorities in America are arbitrarily being categorised because of their physical features, such as their skin colour, hair density, facial features, et cetera.

Therefore, the logical conclusion is that America is capitalistic and that ethnic minorities living in America are suffering from prejudice and the negative effects of power imbalance. Racism is what happens when there is prejudice against ethnic minorities, and what happens when ethnic minorities are suffering the negative consequences of power imbalance.
